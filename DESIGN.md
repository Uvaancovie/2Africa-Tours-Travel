## Overview

The 2Africa Tours & Travel design system is built to convey bespoke luxury, authentic safaris, and high-energy exploration. 
The interface relies strictly on Red, Blue, and White as its core aesthetic drivers.

## Colors

The palette is extremely constrained to maximize contrast and brand recognition.

- **Primary (#1d4ed8):** A deep, vibrant oceanic blue used for dominant brand elements, backgrounds, and headers.
- **Accent (#dc2626):** A striking, high-energy red used strictly for calls-to-action (CTA), prices, and emphasis.
- **Surface (#ffffff):** Pure white used for the foundation to keep the interface breathable and clean.
- **Surface Alt (#f8fafc):** A slightly tinted neutral used for card backgrounds and form inputs to create subtle separation.

## Typography

The system exclusively uses **Poppins**. It is geometric, approachable, and highly legible.

- Headers (h1, h2, h3) use heavy weights (700, 800) for authoritative, bold statements.
- Body text uses a light weight (300) to contrast sharply with the bold headers, bringing a refined, premium editorial feel.

## Shapes

Corners should be rounded to soften the UI and make it feel welcoming and modern. Large cards and imagery use `xl` rounding (`24px`), while buttons and smaller containers use `full` or `md` rounding.
